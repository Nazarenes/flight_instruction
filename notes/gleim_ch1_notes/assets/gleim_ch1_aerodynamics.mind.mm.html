  • Stability
    • Stable - returns to equilibrium after being disturbed
    • center of pressure determines longitudinal stability
    • CG behind CG limit
      • inability to recover from stall
      • less stable at all airspeeds
    • types
      • dynamic
        • Positive
          • decreases in amplitude
        • Neutral
          • neither decreases nor increases in amplitude
          • A worn automobile shock absorber
        • Negative
          • increases and becomes more divergent
      • static
        • Positive
          • tendencey to return to original state of equilibrium
        • Neutral
          • tendency of the aircraft to remain in a new condition
        • Negative
          • tendency of the aircraft to continue away from the original state
    • types
      • Lateral (Rolling)
        • Stability nose to tail

          • Dihedral
          • Sweepback
